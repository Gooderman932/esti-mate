Esti-Mate Pro — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 25, 2026

Developer: Gooderman (Poor Dude Holdings)

Contact: matthew@poordudeholdings.com

1. Information We Collect

Esti-Mate Pro stores all user-created data (customer records, estimates, invoices, measurements, and calibration profiles) locally on your device and optionally synced to our secure cloud database. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

2. Account Information

If you create an account, we store your email address and authentication credentials securely via our cloud provider (Supabase). This information is used solely for account authentication and data synchronization across your devices.

3. Camera Access

The app requests camera permission solely for the Auto Measure feature, which uses your device camera to estimate real-world distances using the pinhole camera model. Camera images are processed locally on your device and are never uploaded, stored on our servers, or transmitted to any third party.

4. Data Storage and Security

Local data is stored in your device's app storage and persists across sessions. Cloud-synced data is stored on encrypted servers provided by Supabase (hosted on Amazon Web Services). Data is transmitted over HTTPS encryption. If you uninstall the app, local data is deleted; cloud data can be deleted by contacting us.

5. Third-Party Services

Esti-Mate Pro uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — secure database and authentication

We do not integrate with any advertising, analytics, or tracking services. No data is shared with advertisers.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is designed for professional construction industry use and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

7. Data Deletion

You can delete all local data by clearing the app's data in your device settings or by uninstalling the app. To request deletion of cloud-stored data, contact us at matthew@poordudeholdings.com.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
